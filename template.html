The Studio

A boutique luxury studio, klove specializes in custom lighting solutions. We create sculptures, installations, chandeliers, pendant lamps, floor lamps, ceiling lights, table lamps, and accessories. Our designs range from neoclassical to contemporary, and are inspired by our travels across India and the world.
